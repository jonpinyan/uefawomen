League Format

Northern Ireland's league is the Women's Premiership, which plays on a summer schedule. Nine teams play a double round-robin (16 games in 18 matchdays). The 2024 season has concluded, with Cliftonville winning the league and qualifying for Round C1 of the 2025-26 UWCL.
