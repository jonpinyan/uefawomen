For the 2024-25 season, Belarus is entitled to two berths in the UWCL. The champion enters at C1, and the runner-up enters at L1.
For the 2025-26 season, Belarus will be entitled to two berths in the UWCL. The champion will enter in Round C2, and the runner-up enters at L2.
For the 2026-27 season, Belarus will be entitled to two berths in European play. The champion will enter the UWCL in Round C2, and the runner-up enters the Second Competition in the first round.

League Format

Belarus's league is the Belarusian Premier League, which plays a summer schedule. Eleven teams play a triple round-robin (30 games in 33 matchdays). The 2024 season is scheduled to end in mid-November.
