League Format

Scotland's league is the SWPL1 (Scottish Women's Premier League, Division 1). Twelve teams play a double round-robin. The top six teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin, for a total of 32 games. For the 2025-26 season, the league will contract to ten teams.
