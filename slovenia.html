For the 2024-25 season, Slovenia is entitled to one berth in the UWCL, with the champion entering at C1.
For the 2025-26 season, Slovenia will be entitled to one berth in the UWCL, with the champion entering in Round C2.
For the 2026-27 season, Slovenia will be entitled to two berths in European play. The champion will enter the UWCL in Round C2, and the runner-up enters the Europa Cup in the first round.

League Format

Slovenia's league is the Women's League. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top five teams advance to the playoffs. The format of the playoffs is unclear. The 2023-24 season had nine teams, with four advancing to a double round-robin playoff.
