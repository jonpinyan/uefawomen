The 2024-25 UEFA Women's Champions League consists of three phases: qualifying rounds, a group stage, and the knockout round. Eight teams compete in the group stage, the teams that finished first and second in their groups during the group stage.
The teams are drawn into a bracket with home-and-away quarterfinals and semifinals, followed by a one-game championship.

Qualified Teams and Draw

Pot 1 contains the seeded teams that won their groups, while Pot 2 contains the unseeded teams that finished second in their groups. For the quarterfinals, the seeded teams host the second leg. There is an open draw for the semifinals. There is no country protection for any part of the knockout stage, but in the quarterfinals, teams cannot be paired up with a team that was in the same group as them for the group stage.
