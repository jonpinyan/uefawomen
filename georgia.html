League Format

Georgia's league is the Women's League, which plays a summer schedule. Eight teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points against the other qualifying teams are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin, for a total of 20 games (12 of them counting in the final standings). For the 2025 season, the league will expand to ten teams. The 2024 season has concluded, with Lanchkhuti winning the league and qualifying for Round C1 of the 2025-26 UWCL.
