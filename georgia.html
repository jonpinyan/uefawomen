League Format

Georgia's league is the Women's League, which plays a summer schedule. Eight teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points against the other qualifying teams are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin. The 2024 playoffs will run through November.
