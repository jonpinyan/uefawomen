League Format

Republic of Ireland's league is the Women's Premier Division, which plays a summer schedule. Eleven teams play a double round-robin (20 games in 22 matchdays). The 2024 season has concluded, with Athlone Town winning the league and qualifying for Round C1 of the 2025-26 UWCL.
