The ranking of women's clubs, for the purpose of seeding the UWCL, is based on the last five years of competitions. This provisional 2025 ranking incorporates results from the 2020-21 through 2024-25 seasons. Each club's coefficient is calculated as either the sum of points earned by the club or 20% of the association's coefficient, whichever is greater. Where clubs are tied, including where multiple clubs from an association use their association's floor ranking, the better ranking goes to the club with more points in the most recent season. The list on this page shows the rankings as they will be calculated once the 2024-25 season concludes and which will likely be used for seeding the 2025-26 competitions; see here for the old calculation method using during the 2024-25 season. Note that the "Last Year" and "Change" columns are based on the 2024 rankings which used the previous system.

Clubs that are still active in the 2024-25 season have their score for that year and club total highlighted. Clubs from an association which has teams still active have their association floor highlighted. Clubs using their association floor have their coefficient highlighted.
