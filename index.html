Qualification for 2024-25

Qualification for the 2024-25 UEFA Women's Champions League is based on the ranking of the associations in the 2023 coefficient list. The top six nations earn three bids, the next ten earn two bids, and all other nations earn one bid.

  • July 5: Draw for Round 1
  • September 4: Round 1 semifinals
  • September 7: Round 1 final and third-place
  • September 8: Draw for Round 2
  • September 18-19: Round 2, leg one
  • September 25-26: Round 2, leg two
  • September 27: Draw for Group Stage
  • October 8-9: Group Stage, matchday one
  • October 16-17: Group Stage, matchday two

  • November 20-21: Group Stage, matchday four
