This site is intended as a collection of data pertaining to the history of the UEFA Women's Champions League, with a focus on the calculation of the coefficients used for ranking associations (and thus, determining how many entrants each nation gets) and clubs (and thus, seeding the teams in the early rounds). While the current data is complete, the historical data is a work in progress.

Club Coefficients for 2025

These coefficients reflect the performance of each club in the UWCL for the seasons 2020-21 through 2024-25. Cells shaded in gold indicate the champions, silver indicates the runner-up, brown and cyan represent semifinalists and quarterfinalists.
Note that, unlike the current version of the men's coefficients, the women's team coefficients are calculated as the points earned by a team plus 20% of the nation's five-year coefficient.
The top 20 teams are listed here. A complete list of teams who have participated in UWCL in the last five years can be found here.

Qualification for 2024-25

Qualification for the 2024-25 UEFA Women's Champions League is based on the ranking of the associations in the 2023 coefficient list. The top six nations earn three bids, the next ten earn two bids, and all other nations earn one bid.

Schedule for 2024-25 UWCL

  • July 5: Draw for Round 1
  • September 4: Round 1 semifinals
  • September 7: Round 1 final and third-place
  • September 8: Draw for Round 2
  • September 18-19: Round 2, leg one
  • September 25-26: Round 2, leg two
  • September 27: Draw for Group Stage
  • October 8-9: Group Stage, matchday one
  • October 16-17: Group Stage, matchday two
  • November 12-13: Group Stage, matchday three
  • November 20-21: Group Stage, matchday four
  • December 11-12: Group Stage, matchday five
  • December 17-18: Group Stage, matchday six
  • February 7: Draw for Knockout Stage
  • March 18-19: Quarterfinals, leg one
  • March 26-27: Quarterfinals, leg two

  • April 26-27: Semifinals, leg two
  • May TBD: UWCL Final in Lisbon
