Team Coefficients for 2024

These coefficients reflect the performance of each nation's teams in the UWCL for the seasons 2019-20 through 2023-24. Cells shaded in gold indicate the champions, silver indicates the runner-up, brown and blue represent semifinalists and quarterfinalists. Bold entries indicate that the team is still in the current competition and so the value may continue to rise.
Note that, unlike the current version of the men's coefficients, the women's team coefficients are calculated as the points earned by a team plus 20% of the nation's five-year coefficient.

Qualification for 2024-25

Qualification for the 2024-25 UEFA Women's Champions League is based on the ranking of the associations in the 2023 coefficient list. Nations are sorted by that metric in the list below. The top six nations earn three bids, the next ten earn two bids, and all other nations earn one bid.

Teams will be listed in the "Qualified" column if they have assured themselves a spot somewhere in the UWCL, but not a specific rank.
