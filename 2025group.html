The 2024-25 UEFA Women's Champions League consists of three phases: qualifying rounds, a group stage, and the knockout round. Sixteen teams compete in the group stage. Four teams have automatic berths, the champions of the top four associations (France - Lyon, Germany - Bayern Munich, Spain - Barcelona, England - Chelsea), with the fourth earning the bid that would be reserved for the defending champions. Seven other national champions earned their way in through qualifying, as did five teams that finished second or third in their league.
The teams will be drawn into four groups of four, playing each other home-and-away for a total of six games. The top two teams from each group advance to the quarterfinals.

Qualified Teams and Draw

The four teams with automatic bids to the group stage are placed in Pot 1. The other 12 teams are placed in Pots 2, 3, and 4 based on their 2024 team coefficient. UEFA divides the four groups into two pairs of two: A and B are red; C and D are blue. For nations with multiple teams in the group stage, two teams are designated to be given different colors, ensuring that the teams will play on different days. If a nation qualifies three teams, the third team is unmatched, but cannot go in the same group as the other teams from that nation. The pairings for this draw, as announced by UEFA are:

  • GER: Bayern Munich and Wolfsburg
  • ITA: Roma and Juventus
  • ENG: Chelsea and Arsenal (Manchester City unmatched)
  • ESP: Barcelona and Real Madrid

Here is the play-by-play of the draw, with additional consequences listed at each step:

  1. Lyon drawn to Group A; Manchester City will be in a blue group
  2. Chelsea drawn to Group B; Arsenal will be in a blue group
  3. Bayern Munich drawn to Group C; Wolfsburg will be in a red group
  4. Barcelona drawn to Group D; Real Madrid will be in a red group
  5. Arsenal drawn to Group C; Manchester City will be in Group D
  6. Real Madrid drawn to Group B; Wolfsburg will be in Group A
  7. Roma drawn to Group A; Juventus will be in a blue group
  8. Twente drawn to Group B
  9. Juventus drawn to Group C; St. Polten will be in Group D
  10. Galatasaray drawn to Group A
  11. Celtic drawn to Group B
  12. Hammarby drawn to Group D; Valerenga will be in Group C
