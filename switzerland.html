League Format

Switzerland's league is the Women's Super League. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top eight teams advance to the playoffs. The playoffs are on a knockout system with home-and-away quarterfinals and semifinals, followed by a one-game final, for a toal of 23 games. In 2022, the only year under the current schedule format with two Swiss teams qualifying for European play, the second berth was awarded to the regular season champion (according to Wikipedia), though that team was also the playoff runner-up.
