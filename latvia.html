RFS Women

Currently playing in the Women's League. Originally competed as Rigas Futbola skola (Rigas FS / Riga Football School). In 2021, the club spun off SFK Riga, which UEFA considers to be a continuation of the original club, even though a separate team continues to play in the Latvian top league as Rigas FS. In 2024, SFK Riga changed its name to RFS Women. 2025 Coefficient: 6.5 + 1.300 = 7.800.

League Format

Latvia's league is the Women's League, which plays on a summer schedule. Eight teams play a triple round-robin (21 games). The 2024 season has concluded, with Riga FC winning the league and qualifying for Round C1 of the 2025-26 UWCL.
