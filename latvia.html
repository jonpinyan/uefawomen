Full History by Team

RFS Women

Originally competed as Rigas Futbola skola (Rigas FS / Riga Football School). In 2021, the club spun off SFK Riga, which UEFA considers to be a continuation of the original club, even though a separate team continues to play in the Latvian top league as Rigas FS. In 2024, SFK Riga changed its name to RFS Women.

League Format

Latvia's league is the Women's League, which plays on a summer schedule. Eight teams play a triple round-robin. The 2024 season runs through November 10.
