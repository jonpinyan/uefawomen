League Format

Faroe Islands' league is the Deildin kvinnur, which plays a summer schedule. Eight teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin. The 2024 season has concluded, with NSI winning the league and qualifying for Round C1 of the 2025-26 UWCL.
