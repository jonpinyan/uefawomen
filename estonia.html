League Format

Estonia's league is the Naiste Meistriliiga, which plays on a summer schedule. Eight teams play a triple round-robin. The top six teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional round-robin. The 2024 regular season will end in October, with the playoffs to follow through November.
