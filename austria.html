For the 2024-25 season, Austria is entitled to two berths in the UWCL. The champion enters at C1, and the runner-up enters at L1.
For the 2025-26 season, Austria will be entitled to three berths in European play. The champion will enter the UWCL in Round C2, the runner-up enters the UWCL at L2, and the third place team enters the Second Competition in the first round.
For the 2026-27 season, Austria will be entitled to three berths in European play. The champion will enter the UWCL in Round C2, the runner-up enters the UWCL at L2, and the third place team's entry is pending.

League Format

Austria's league is the Women's Bundesliga. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The 2024-25 season is underway and is scheduled to conclude in March 2025.
