For the 2025-26 season, Sweden will be entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion enters at Round C2, and the runner-up and third-place team enter at Round L2.
For the 2026-27 season, Sweden will be entitled to three berths in the UWCL. Where the teams being play is pending.
