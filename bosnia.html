League Format

Bosnia and Herzegovina's league is the Women's Premier League. Eight teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin. The 2024-25 regular season will end in March, with the playoffs to follow in April and May.
