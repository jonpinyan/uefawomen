For the 2024-25 season, Albania is entitled to one berth in the UWCL, with the champion entering at C1.
For the 2025-26 season, Albania will be entitled to two berths in European play. The champion will enter the UWCL in Round C1, and the runner-up will enter the Second Competition in the first round.
For the 2026-27 season, Albania will be entitled to two berths in the UWCL. The champion will enter in Round C2, and the runner-up enters at L2.

League Format

Albania's league is the Kampionati Kombetar Futboll Femra. Ten teams play a double round-robin, with the top four advancing to the playoffs. The playoffs are on a knockout system with home-and-away semifinals, followed by one-game championship and bronze finals. The 2024-25 season begins September 27.
