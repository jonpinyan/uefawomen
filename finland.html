League Format

Finland's league is the Kansallinen Liga, which plays on a summer schedule. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin. The 2024 season has concluded, with HJK (1st, UWCL C2) and KuPS (2nd, second competition) qualifying for European play.
