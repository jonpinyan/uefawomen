League Format

Finland's league is the Kansallinen Liiga, which plays on a summer schedule. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top four teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin, for a total of 24 games. The 2024 season has concluded, with HJK (Champions, UWCL Round C2) and KuPS (runner-up, Europa Cup) qualifying for European play.
