For the 2024-25 season, Germany is entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion enters the group stage, the runner-up enters round L2, and the third-place team enters L1.
For the 2025-26 season, Germany will be entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion and runner-up enter the league stage, and the third-place team enters in Round L3.
For the 2026-27 season, Germany will be entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion enters the league stage; where the other two enter is pending.

League Format

Germany's league is the Frauen-Bundesliga. Twelve teams play a double round-robin. In the 2025-26 season, the league will expand to fourteen teams.
