League Format

Italy's league is Serie A. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top five teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional double round-robin. for a total of 26 games in 28 matchdays. In the 2025-26 season, the league will expand to twelve teams.
