League Format

Iceland's league is the Besta deild kvenna, which plays on a summer schedule. Ten teams play a double round-robin. The top six teams advance to the playoffs. Regular season points are carried over, and then teams play an additional round-robin, for a total of 28 games. The 2024 season has concluded, with Breidablik (1st, C2) and Valur (2nd, L2) qualifying for the 2025-26 UWCL.
