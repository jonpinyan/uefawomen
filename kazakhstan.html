League Format

Kazakhstan's league is the women's championship, which plays on a summer schedule. Five teams play a quadruple round-robin. The 2024 season has concluded, with BIIK Shymkent (1st, C2) and Aktobe (2nd, L2) qualifying for the 2025-26 UWCL.
