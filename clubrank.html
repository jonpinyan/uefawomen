The ranking of women's clubs, for the purpose of seeding the UWCL, is based on the last five years of competitions. The current 2025 ranking incorporates results from the 2020-21 through 2024-25 seasons. Each club's coefficient is calculated as the sum of points earned by the club, plus 20% of the association's coefficient. However, effective with the 2025-26 season, the club coefficient will be either the sum of points earned by the club or 20% of the association's coefficient, whichever is greater. The list on this page shows the rankings as they were calculated during the 2024-25 season; see here for the new calculation method which will likely be used for seeding the 2025-26 competitions.

Teams that are still active in the 2024-25 season have their score for that year and club total highlighted. Teams from an association which has teams still active have their association contribution highlighted.
