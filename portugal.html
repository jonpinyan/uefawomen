For the 2024-25 season, Portugal is entitled to two berths in the UWCL, with the champion entering at C1 and the runner-up entering at L1.
For the 2025-26 season, Portugal is entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion enters the league stage, the runner-up enters in Round L3, and the third-place team enters at L2.
For the 2026-27 season, Portugal is entitled to three berths in the UWCL. The champion enters the league stage, and the second and third-place teams enter in Round L2.

League Format

Portugal's league is the Campeonato Nacional. Twelve teams play a double round-robin, for a total of 22 games. In 2025-26, the league will contract to ten teams.
